Admission process is fast and easy. Interested parents need to walk in to the school with their child on any working day between 9:00 a.m. and 1:00 p.m. They are then given a campus tour after which they can finish the admission formalities by filling the form and paying the relevant fees. Please note: 1. It is mandatory that parents visit with their child for the admission inquiry. 2. Yashshrri doesn’t conduct any interviews/ tests for parents/ students for admissions.
